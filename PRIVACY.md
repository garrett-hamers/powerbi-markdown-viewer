# Privacy Policy

## Power BI Markdown Viewer Visual

**Effective Date:** February 2026

### Data Collection

This Power BI custom visual does **not** collect, store, or transmit any personal data or user information.

### Data Processing

- All markdown content is processed locally within the Power BI environment
- No data is sent to external servers
- No cookies or tracking mechanisms are used
- No analytics or telemetry is collected

### Third-Party Services

This visual does not integrate with any third-party services or APIs.
